Why Functional Mushrooms?

Humans have been turning to mushrooms for centuries. Across cultures, people noticed that certain fungi supported functional benefits including energy, endurance, focus, clarity, and stress relief.

Today, science is helping to explain why fungi have held our attention for so long. Reishi

Stress Relief & Calm

What it's known for:
Reishi has a rich history in traditional wellness systems for promoting calm, immune balance, and resilience to stress. Modern research supports its role in supporting antioxidant activity, immune-response modulation, and overall wellness.

Key compounds:

Energy & Endurance

Monterey Nutra enhances our mushroom extracts through a proprietary dual-extraction process that maximizes bioactive compound retention:
