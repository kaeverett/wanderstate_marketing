Nature Brings Out the Wander in You

Science keeps proving what we feel the second we step outside: time in nature lowers stress, boosts focus, eases anxiety, and helps your brain reset.

A few minutes of fresh air can do more for your mood than another scroll through your feed. That's the spirit behind Wander State — simple, natural tools that help you feel more you.

Disconnect to Reconnect

We spend more time staring at screens than staring at trees — and we feel it.

Being in nature makes it easier to settle your mind and reconnect with yourself. Wander State is made to pair with the outdoors — a simple way to support your connection to nature, yourself, and each other.

The Places We Support

More Trails, More Wandering — Washington Trails Association

Wander State was born in Washington State, and we still call it home.

The forests, coastlines, mountains, and rolling hills here shaped who we are — and continue to inspire everything we make.

That's why we support the Washington Trails Association, the folks caring for over 3,000 miles of paths that lead to alpine lakes, ridge-line views, and forests that smell like pine and possibility. They're out there keeping the wild places wander-ready — one switchback, boardwalk, and trail sign at a time.

Parks Are Good for the Soul — National Park Service

National parks are nature's greatest hits: giant trees, wild coastlines, canyon walls, starry skies, and the kind of views that remind you life is bigger than your inbox.

The National Park Service protects 85 million acres of awe-filled places across the country, and we're proud to support their work.

Every can of Wander State helps keep those parks open, protected, and ready for whatever kind of day you need — from a quick wander to a big adventure.
