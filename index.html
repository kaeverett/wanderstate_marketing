Our Story

Wander State began in Seattle in 2014, rooted in a friendship built on shared values, mutual respect, and a desire to create something meaningful. At the time, Tommy was leading operations and reimagining the beverage program at a local pizza chain, while Eric was at the helm of Jones Soda. When the two partnered to replace Coke with Jones as the chain's local soda partner, it was a small but telling move‚Äîit confirmed they saw brand, business, and relationships through the same lens.

As the years passed, the idea of building something together kept resurfacing. Eventually, life lined up. Over a beer at a neighborhood brewery, Tommy pitched Eric on starting a beverage company‚Äîone centered around functional mushrooms.

About Tommy

Tommy brings over a decade of leadership in the food and beverage industry, where he built and scaled beverage programs for multi-unit restaurant groups and cultivated deep relationships with brands, distributors, and operators. His background blends operational expertise with sharp consumer insight‚Äîparticularly into the evolving demand for wellness-driven, functional beverages.

That professional path runs parallel to a personal one. During a difficult period, Tommy began exploring functional mushrooms as a tool for mental clarity, physical performance, and emotional grounding. They worked‚Äîand quickly became essential to his wellness routine. That lived experience, paired with years of experience designing what ends up on menus and shelves, revealed a clear white space: today's consumers want more from what they drink. They want clean, purposeful ingredients. Products that deliver tangible benefit. And they want to feel connected‚Äîto what they're putting in their bodies and the world around them.

Tommy's passion for health, nature, and human connection is deeply embedded in Wander State's mission. Raised in California's Bay Area, he was early to wellness‚Äîprioritizing whole foods and environmental activism long before either were mainstream. A lifelong athlete, he now competes in trail races as a sponsored runner for the Seattle Brooks Running Club, and continues to spend as much time in nature as possible.

To Tommy, nature is medicine. And connection‚Äîto self, to others, and to the planet‚Äîis at the heart of what it means to be well.

While functional mushroom beverages existed, they felt limited‚Äîoften clinical, inaccessible, or uninspired. We envisioned something more: a product that was approachable, intentional, and effective. Easy to grab for specific dayparts or activities. Rooted in functionality, but designed to spark curiosity, invite connection, and act as a gateway to nature and community.

From day one, Wander State has been about connection‚Äîto ourselves, each other, and nature. This journey has deepened our relationships with our families, friends, and partners. Our hope is that it helps others feel more connected too.

Wherever your path takes you, we're grateful to wander with you.
